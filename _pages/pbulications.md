# Research & Publications

My research background is in materials science, X-ray spectroscopy, machine learning, optimisation, and scientific software development.

My work mainly focuses on depth-resolved X-ray spectroscopy, data-efficient experimental design, machine learning for scientific workflows, and analytical software for complex experimental systems.
